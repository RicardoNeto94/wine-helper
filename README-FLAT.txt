All files are flat in this folder.
To add local bottle images, just upload PNGs here named:
  <slug>-<vintage>.png  or  <slug>.png
(slug = lower-case wine name, accents removed, spaces -> hyphens)

Example:
  louis-roederer-cristal-2014.png
  dom-perignon.png

The app also reads remote URLs from images-map.json.
